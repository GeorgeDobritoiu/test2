Line Up


The Swinging Notes

The Swinging Notes: More than just a name, it's a feeling. We're a passionate group of musicians dedicated to keeping the spirit of swing alive. Expect toe-tapping classics, dazzling improvisations, and a whole lot of fun!

Seasoned veterans of the jazz scene, The Swinging Notes bring a wealth of experience and musical expertise to every stage. We weave together timeless melodies with modern influences, creating a sound that's both nostalgic and fresh.

The Swinging Notes: Injecting infectious energy and classic cool into every performance. Get ready to tap your feet and move to the groove!

Miles High Quartet

Hailing from New York, the Miles High Quartet takes listeners on a captivating journey through the world of jazz. This tight-knit ensemble, comprised of seasoned musicians with a deep love for the genre, blends tradition with modern influences to create a truly unique sound.

Witnessing the Miles High Quartet live is an unforgettable experience. Their energy and passion are contagious, and their improvisational skills are second to none. Each performance is a unique adventure, a testament to the power of spontaneous creativity in jazz.

Get ready to be swept away by the soaring improvisations and infectious energy of the Miles High Quartet!

Blue Note Trio

The Blue Note Trio is a tight-knit ensemble that breathes new life into the timeless sounds of classic jazz. Influenced by the legendary artists who frequented the iconic Blue Note record label, this trio delivers a captivating blend of swing, bop, and cool jazz with a touch of modern flair.

The Blue Note Trio's repertoire includes beloved jazz standards reimagined with a fresh perspective. They also incorporate lesser-known gems, uncovering hidden treasures from the vast landscape of jazz history. Expect to hear familiar tunes you love, alongside exciting improvisational journeys that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
