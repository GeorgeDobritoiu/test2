Welcome to the Jazz Festival!

We're excited to have you join us for a day filled with fantastic music, fun activities, and a relaxed atmosphere. This festival is perfect for the whole family, including your furry friends!

What to Bring

  • Blankets or lawn chairs to relax on
  • Picnic basket with your favorite food and drinks
  • Sunscreen and hats (optional, depending on weather)

**Please note:** Barbecues or open flames are not permitted on festival grounds.

Family Fun

We have a dedicated kid's area with fun activities to keep your little ones entertained throughout the festival.

Noise-canceling headsets are available for rent to help protect children's hearing during the festival.

Important Information

  • The festival is dog-friendly, but please keep your furry friends leashed at all times.
  • Restrooms and concession stands are located throughout the festival grounds.

Rules & Regulations

General

  • **Admission:** A valid ticket is required for entry.
  • **Festival Hours:** The festival operates from 11 AM to 11 PM.
  • **Lost and Found:** Lost and Found is located at INFO KIOSC. Please report any lost items to a staff member.
  • **Restrooms:** Restrooms are located throughout the festival grounds.

Safety

  • **Children:** Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.
  • **Medical Attention:** First aid stations are located at INFO KIOSC. Please notify a staff member or security personnel in case of any medical emergency.
  • **Lost Children:** If you lose sight of your child, please report it immediately to a staff member or security personnel. Announcements will be made over the public address system.
  • **Prohibited Items:**
    * Glass bottles or containers
    * Alcohol (unless purchased from authorized vendors)
    * Illegal substances
    * Fireworks or explosives
    * Weapons of any kind
    * Bicycles, skateboards, scooters (except for designated areas)
    * Barbecues or open flames

Grounds

  • **Smoking:** Smoking is only permitted in designated areas. Please dispose of cigarette butts responsibly.
  • **Parking:** Parking is available at EAST SIDE ENTRANCE for a fee. Please follow all posted parking signs and instructions.
  • **Accessibility:** Accessible restrooms and designated seating areas are available for guests with disabilities. Please inquire with a staff member for assistance.
  • **Lost and Found:** Lost and Found is located at INFO KIOSC. Please report any lost items to a staff member.

Thank you for cooperating and helping to make the Jazz Festival a fun and memorable experience for everyone!
