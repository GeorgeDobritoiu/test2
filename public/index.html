Headlining the First Note: Welcome to Jazz'a'Bella!

Experience a Day Filled with Unforgettable Jazz:

Get ready to immerse yourself in the world of captivating melodies and rhythmic grooves at the inaugural Jazz'a'Bella festival! This one-day event, meticulously crafted by a team boasting 20 years of festival expertise, promises an unforgettable journey through the heart of jazz music.

Three Stages, Endless Possibilities:

Immerse yourself in a vibrant atmosphere with music erupting from three distinct stages, each showcasing a unique selection of genres within the vast jazz landscape. From the electrifying energy of swing to the soulful tones of contemporary jazz, Jazz'a'Bella caters to every jazz enthusiast's taste.

13 Bands, One Unforgettable Day

Prepare to be enthralled by the talent of 13 exceptional bands gracing our stages. Witness the magic unfold as renowned artists and rising stars alike captivate the audience with their musical prowess.

More Than Just Music, A Festival for All:

Indulge in delectable treats from our diverse food court, offering a variety of culinary options to tantalize your taste buds.

We haven't forgotten the little ones either! A dedicated kid's area provides a haven for fun activities, ensuring a day of entertainment for the whole family.

Bring along your furry companion! Jazz'a'Bella welcomes well-behaved pets on leashes to share in the festival spirit.

Join Us for the Premiere:

Mark your calendars and prepare to be swept away by the magic of Jazz'a'Bella! This inaugural event promises an unparalleled celebration of jazz music, leaving you wanting more.

Visit our website for updates on the performing artists, festival schedule, and ticketing information. Don't miss your chance to be a part of this historic event!
